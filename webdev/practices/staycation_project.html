Arches National Park

The Arches of the Park

Arches National Park is a home to over 2,000 natural stone arches, which formed due to erosion. This place is crazy unique and naturally beautiful. You can visit arches like the world-famous Delicate Arch. The distinct red and orange color of the rock will make your visit here unforgettable. Arches is also a very famous spot at night, where the night sky is very bright and clear, where you can see every star and constellation. Overall, Arches is like nature's New York City, with every arch having it's own unique features to it.

Visiting this park is like stepping into a desert rock playgroud, all of which you can hike and explore. There are hikes are all types of people. There are nice scenic family hikes like the window arches, and long difficult hikes like Fiery Furnace for more experienced hikers. There is also much more to do here than just hike. You can try canyoneering, which involves rock climbing and repelling down narrow slots in the rock formations. Another thing to do here that is popular is mountain biking. There are plenty of easy routes and plenty of double black diamonds for everyone to enjoy. This park is overall very friendly to everyone. There is truly an endless amount of things to do at Arches National Park.

The Experiences of the Park

Reasons to visit Arches National Park

  • There are plenty of iconic landmarks to see. Some include the famous Delicate Arch, the Double Arch, and the Landscape Arch.
  • There are plenty of scenic and easy hikes, most of which are around an hour long. This makes this destination perfect for people of all skills, tourists, families, and hiking pros because there are all types of hikes.
  • The geography here is unlike the rest of the world. Nowhere else on the planet is there this many natural arches in such a small area. This makes every arch/destination very accessible.
  • There is many other physical activites like mountain biking and canyoneering that everyone enjoys, even beginners. There are experienced guides who will lead you through every step to mastering mountain biking and canyoneering, so no need to fear!
